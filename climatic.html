Future (2020 to 2100)

These are downscaled future climate projection from different GCMs based on CMIP6. Bias correction and downscaling was done with WorldClim 2.1 as baseline climate. It includes monthly average minimum temperature, monthly average maximum temperature, monthly total precipitation, and bioclimatic variables at 4 different scenarios: SSP1-2.6, SSP2-4.5, SSP3-7.0 and SSP5-8.5. Read more about CMIP6 here. Take note that the bioclimatic variables and monthly variables are stored in band composites. You can separate each band to a raster file.
